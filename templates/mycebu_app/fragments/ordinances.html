Cebu City Ordinances

Official and consolidated list of city laws, resolutions, and local legislation.

  • Ordinance No. 2345 (Traffic and Mobility)

    **Enacted:** January 15, 2023

    Regulating the speed and flow of vehicles within the city proper to alleviate traffic congestion.

  • Ordinance No. 2346 (Waste Management)

    **Enacted:** March 1, 2023

    Implementing mandatory waste segregation at the source and penalizing non-compliance.

  • Ordinance No. 2347 (Digital Citizen ID)

    **Enacted:** May 20, 2023

    Establishing the electronic MyCebu Citizen ID for streamlined government transactions.
